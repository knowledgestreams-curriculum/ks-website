    About Us

    Vision

    To become the provider of choice of trained professionals for the IT industry in Pakistan. KnowlEDGE Streams aims to bridge the talent – employment gap in the IT sector at scale, with speed, and quality, exceeding international standards.

    Value Proposition

    KnowlEDGE Streams takes on the responsibility of training manpower for the IT Industry, thereby significantly reducing involvement of a company’s space, time and senior resources allocated for trainings. Highest quality of industry-experienced instructors, relevance and efficacy of an industry-driven curriculum, with flawless execution at speed and scale, ensuring success.
