Dr. Ishrat visits Knowledge Streams

Renowned Pakistani Economist, Dr. Ishrat Hussain, made a special visit to our campus that left a lasting impact. As the HEC Pakistan National Distinguished Professor, Professor Emeritus, and Chairman of the Centre for Excellence in Islamic Finance at Institute of Business Administration Karachi, Dr. Hussain brought a wealth of knowledge and experience with him.

Speaking to the trainees on the need for competition and collaboration, Dr. Hussain encouraged the students to strive for excellence and reminded them of the importance of teamwork and collaboration in the workplace.

It was a interaction filled with insights, inspiration, and education. We're grateful to have had the opportunity to host Dr. Ishrat Hussain!
