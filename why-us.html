Why Us

Background

Qualified manpower represents the key competitive advantage for any organization operating in the IT sector. While Pakistan is a country of 220 million people, 50%of whom are less than 25, it is estimated that less than 30,000 students graduate in IT related disciplines each year; of which only a small fraction meet industry standards, as key problems faced by the local industry are deficiencies in technical areas such as programming, databases and software engineering principles, combined with a lack of soft skills, especially in business communications, personal leadership and teamwork. These deficiencies greatly impact the time it takes for a fresh inductee to become productive or “billable.”

These IT graduates are a small fraction of all two and four year program graduates of colleges and universities in mathematics-based fields such as engineering, statistics, mathematics, economics, accounting, etc. Graduates of such programs are mostly under-employed and represent a large reservoir of ready talent that, given the advances in learning to application development, can be provided requisite technical support to acquire knowledge and skills invaluable for the IT industry. The lack of soft skills is a common theme for these graduates as well.

A significant local and global opportunity exists for high quality IT qualified human resource that is “market ready” or “billable.”

Vision

To become the provider of choice of trained professionals for the IT industry in Pakistan. KnowlEDGE Streams aims to bridge the talent – employment gap in the IT sector at scale, with speed, and quality, exceeding international standards.

Value Proposition

KnowlEDGE Streams takes on the responsibility of training manpower for the IT Industry, thereby significantly reducing involvement of a company’s space, time and senior resources allocated for trainings.

Highest quality of industry-experienced instructors, relevance and efficacy of an industry-driven curriculum, with flawless execution at speed and scale, ensuring success

Environment & Service Offerings

We have a dedicated corporate training facility in Lahore. Planning expansion to Karachi and other cities.

  • Technical sessions with Hands-on training in workspaces having computer workstation clusters to facilitate group learning. Facilitated by teaching assistants.
  • Trainees expected to follow norms of appearance, manners, interactions, as expected in a corporate facility.
  • Detailed multidimensional assessment of trainee performance will be available

3 month face-to-face programs. The Curriculum consists of three modules:

  • Module-1 (Software Engineering Fundamentals): Builds a common baseline among those coming from different IT backgrounds/programs.
  • Modules-2 (Technical Stream): Builds technical skills in a specific area, such as MERN, .NET, Python/DJANGO, etc. Soft skills development in core areas of communication, leadership and teamwork
  • Module-3 (Soft Skills): Runs concurrently with Modules 1, 2 and focuses on building professionalism of trainees as applicable to the IT industry in particular

Final capstone project, chosen in consultation with the industry

Outcomes

High quality, trained manpower for the IT industry allowing the sector to significantly expand its local and global presence, while providing opportunities for the talented youth to develop and prosper.
