Emblem becomes Knowledge Partner

Pakistan's IT sector doesn't lack talent but it always needed someone to fill the gap between the skills of fresh graduates and the requirements of the IT industry. KnowlEDGE Streams took the initiative to fill the gap and Emblem takes pride in contributing to the initial batch of grads, who'd be able to develop softskills and polish their emotional and social skills to meet the requirements of the tech industry.

On Thursday, the Emblem team visited the KnowlEDGE Streams campus to mark the six-week success of the first batch of the pilot program. We were delighted to see the program development and spend time with candidates, hearing their personal views regarding the initiative and how it's helping them prepare them for their professional lives.

On this occasion, another agreement was signed between Emblem and KnowlEDGE Streams. Both entities are now Knowledgepartners in the furtherance of the following goals:

  • Focus on self improvement of fresh graduates to bridge the talent-employment gap in IT sector
  • Focus on self improvement of fresh graduates to bridge the talent-employment gap in IT sector
  • Mentor all trainees
  • Provide focused training in industry verticals of interest
  • Provide experienced employees as part-time instructors for deeper engagement
